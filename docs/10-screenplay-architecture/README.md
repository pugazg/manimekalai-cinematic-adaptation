## 10B result

The 154 registered scene concepts are now classified as:

- 61 `KEEP` principal feature scenes;
- 11 `BRANCH` embedded-story beats;
- 69 `MERGE` concepts absorbed into named surviving scenes;
- 10 `RESERVE` concepts for an extended cut or alternate structure;
- 3 `OMIT` concepts retained only as research and production texture.

The working feature backbone therefore contains **72 active screenplay units** within the existing 165-minute ten-sequence architecture.

## Architecture rules

- The feature remains Manimekalai's story even when branch narratives occupy the screen.
- Every branch story must alter a present decision, relationship or institution.
- The poem's chapter order may be compressed or intercut, but knowledge order and causal order cannot be falsified.
- Supernatural events create vulnerability, knowledge or responsibility; they are not consequence-free spectacle.
- A merged scene inherits relevant evidence and binding adaptation decisions from every absorbed concept.
- Reserve material may return only after runtime and continuity consequences are recalculated.
- Omitted standalone scenes remain valid research references but are not events in the working screenplay.
- The 165-minute target remains a ceiling for the first treatment.
- Philosophy must appear through inquiry, inference, material consequence and practice.
- No dialogue draft begins before the 10C beat sheets reconcile all 72 active units.
