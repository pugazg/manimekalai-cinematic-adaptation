# Tamil Terminology and Character-Cue Policy

## Purpose

This policy separates editorial consistency from specialist authority. A spelling or cue may be locked for the bilingual screenplay while the historical, philological or institutional interpretation behind a term remains open.

## Locked editorial rules

1. Tamil screenplay character cues use Tamil script.
2. English character cues remain in the English master only.
3. `(CONT'D)` is rendered as `(தொடர்ச்சி)`.
4. The locked Tamil character forms in `translation_guidelines.md` govern the screenplay.
5. The Queen's name is `இராசமாதேவி`; `ராஜமாதேவி` and `ராசமாதேவி` are prohibited in Tamil screenplay files.
6. Both English spellings `DEEPATHILAKAI` and the inherited typo `DEEPATHILAHAI` map to the single Tamil cue `தீவதிலகை`.
7. Generic roles are translated by dramatic function rather than transliterated.

The bilingual validator enforces Rules 1 and 5. These are editorial controls, not claims about the only philologically possible spelling in every edition.

## Locked principal forms

`மணிமேகலை`, `மாதவி`, `சுதமதி`, `சித்திராபதி`, `உதயகுமரன்`, `அறவண அடிகள்`, `தீவதிலகை`, `ஆபுத்திரன்`, `ஆதிரை`, `காயசண்டிகை`, `காஞ்சனன்`, `இராசமாதேவி`, `புண்ணியராசன்`, `அமுதசுரபி`, `மணிபல்லவம்`, `கோமுகி`, `உலக அறவி`, `புகார்`.

## Specialist-open terminology

The cue conversion does not settle these questions:

- the exact historical offices, ranks or institutional meanings behind `அறவண அடிகள்` and other renunciant labels;
- whether `ஆசீவக`, `நிகண்டவாத`, `வைசேடிக`, `பூதவாதி` and related school names require different forms in dialogue, scholarship and subtitles;
- whether the modern explanatory label `பொருள்முதல்வாதி` should remain anywhere beside `பூதவாதி`;
- the best Tamil for causation, inference, proof and dependent origination;
- period-appropriate language for custody, testimony, public record, prison reform and relief administration;
- the historical and culinary interpretation of `சோறு`, `கறி` and `பசுநெய்` in the approved Aadhirai passage;
- the social and religious range of `இந்திர விழா`, which remains an early Tamil civic-sacral institution and must not be automatically equated with later devotional iconography.

These remain visible in `tamil-terminology-register.csv` until the relevant specialist gate is completed.

## Performance rule

Editorial consistency does not grant dialogue lock. Tamil cues and spellings may be correct while a line still requires actor-led rhythm, breath, interruption or register review.
