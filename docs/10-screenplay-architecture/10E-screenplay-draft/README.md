# 10E — Screenplay Draft 0.1

## Working screenplay edition

**Project:** *Manimekalai* cinematic adaptation  
**Format:** Fountain-compatible plain text  
**Working dialogue language:** English  
**Intended production language:** Tamil  
**Architecture:** 72 approved units, 10 sequences, 165-minute ceiling

This folder converts the approved 10D treatment into scene headings, action and dialogue.

The English dialogue is provisional adaptation writing. It is not a translation of the source poem and must not be treated as final Tamil dialogue. Exact Tamil philosophical, ritual, caste-historical, geographical and institutional terminology requires specialist review before a Tamil dialogue pass.

## Fountain conventions

- Scene headings use standard `INT.`, `EXT.` and `INT./EXT.` forms.
- Scene numbers match the approved 10B/10C unit order, from `#1#` through `#72#`.
- Fountain boneyard comments such as `/* TRACE: ... */` are non-printing audit metadata.
- A trace comment identifies the primary feature unit, primary `SC-*` record and absorbed scene concepts.
- Memory and revelation scenes are clearly marked in headings or transitions.
- Dialogue is deliberately restrained. The first draft privileges action, visual causation and character choices over explanatory speeches.

## Draft rules

1. Manimekalai remains the protagonist.
2. Previous-life continuity never cancels present consent, identity or duty.
3. Miracles create responsibility and vulnerability rather than effortless victory.
4. Hunger relief remains dependent on labour, water, access, sanitation and recipient knowledge.
5. Udayakumaran's sincerity never creates consent.
6. Kanchanan's grief and mistaken recognition never remove responsibility for lethal action.
7. Rajamadevi's grief never conceals the institutional nature of custody, drugging, defamation or starvation.
8. Rival philosophical positions receive intelligible arguments rather than caricature.
9. The final relief system continues beyond Manimekalai's individual hands.
10. No exact historical reconstruction is treated as certain where the research archive marks it disputed.

## Draft status

Draft 0.1 is a source-controlled dramatic construction for internal review. It is not yet a shooting script, dialogue-locked screenplay, production draft or authorised Tamil-language version.
