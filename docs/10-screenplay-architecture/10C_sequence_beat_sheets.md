# 10C — Sequence Beat Sheets

## Feature Architecture Edition 0.1

**Project:** *Manimekalai* cinematic adaptation  
**Input:** 72 active units from 10B  
**Working runtime:** 165 minutes

## Governing rule

Every beat must change at least one of: objective, knowledge, relationship, institutional condition or ethical responsibility. Branch beats enter only through a present-tense question and exit by changing the present.

# SEQ-01 — The City Before the Storm

**Runtime:** 15 minutes  
**Dramatic question:** Who gets to define Manimekalai before she speaks?

| Order | Unit | Beat | Objective | Reversal / knowledge change | Exit state |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|

| 4 | `BR-001` | Madhavi Brings the Unspeakable | Root renunciation in Kovalan's death and received grief. | Aravana listens before interpreting. | Grief becomes the hidden cause of the household break. |
