## Decision rationale

The selected revision preserves the strongest source-supported ethical and causal order:

1. household labour prepares food;
2. Aadhirai voluntarily opens private resources;
3. she materially fills the vessel rather than contributing only a symbol;
4. Manimekalai serves actual recipients;
5. continuing abundance is then discovered;
6. water, access, queues, translation, cleaning and shared labour remain necessary.

This is stronger than the current handful of grain because it makes Aadhirai's contribution material and prevents the miraculous vessel from appearing to generate the entire first fullness by itself.

It is more responsible than immediately locking cooked rice, curry and ghee into screenplay action because the checked primary witness supports food, offering and fullness but does not yet securely establish every culinary detail supplied by Bharathidasan.
