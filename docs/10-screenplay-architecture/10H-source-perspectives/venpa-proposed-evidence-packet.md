# Venpa Proposed Evidence Packet

## Status

**Scope:** the thirteen `PROPOSED` records in the Venpa master decision register  
**Screenplay text changed:** No  
**TRACE records changed:** No  
**Purpose:** prepare source verification before any approve, revise or reject decision

This packet records exact e-text heading anchors and preliminary primary-epic counterparts. It does **not** satisfy the scan-page verification gate. The available Project Madurai text identifies its printed source as Bharathidasan, *Manimekalai Venpa*, Pari Nilayam, 1990, but the UTF-8 e-text does not preserve stable printed page numbers.

Therefore each record distinguishes:

- **E-text anchor found** — exact heading or uniquely identifiable movement located;
- **Printed page pending** — must be resolved from the Tamil Virtual Academy scan or a lawfully held printed copy;
- **Primary counterpart** — preliminary chapter or line anchor in the epic, where located;
- **Evidence class** — direct, thematic or combined support.
