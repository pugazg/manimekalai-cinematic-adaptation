# 05B — Knowledge, Debate and Reasoning

## Philosophy Bible Edition 0.1

## Manimekalai as examiner

Manimekalai's journey through philosophical schools is not a contest of defeating opponents.

She listens before judging.

The debate sequence must preserve intellectual dignity.

---

## Dramatic structure

### Vanji — listening

Different schools present different answers about:

- knowledge;
- soul;
- matter;
- causation;
- liberation.

### Kanchi — testing

Arguments become connected to reasoning methods.

The audience sees how conclusions are formed.

### Final teaching — practice

Truth is measured through transformation.

---

## Screen rule

Do not create a modern debate show.

The scene should feel like a living intellectual city:

- teachers;
- students;
- patrons;
- listeners;
- disagreement without violence.

---

## Material stakes

A philosophy that cannot answer hunger, grief and suffering remains incomplete for the cinematic journey.
