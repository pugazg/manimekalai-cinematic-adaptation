# 05C — Ethics, Hunger and Liberation

## Philosophy Bible Edition 0.1

## Giving food is a philosophical statement

The epic does not separate spiritual life from physical suffering.

Food, care and dignity are philosophical practice.

---

## Amudhasurabhi principle

The vessel teaches:

- abundance is not ownership;
- service is not status;
- compassion requires organisation.

---

## Karma and responsibility

The film must distinguish:

- beliefs characters hold about karma;
- the immediate responsibility to relieve suffering.

Karma must never become an excuse to ignore hunger.

---

## Liberation

Freedom means release from destructive attachment while remaining responsive to life.

Manimekalai's final state is not indifference.

It is compassionate clarity.
