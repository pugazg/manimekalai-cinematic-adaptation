# Adaptation Risk Register

_Last updated: 2026-07-21_

This register consolidates risks already distributed across the research, character, philosophy, screenplay and review documents. It does not replace their detailed reasoning.

## Scales

- **Likelihood:** Low / Medium / High
- **Impact:** Moderate / Major / Critical
- **State:** Open / Controlled / Blocked / Accepted

## Review cadence

Update this register when:

- a new evidence source changes an adaptation assumption;
- a Venpa proposal is approved, revised or rejected;
- a specialist review closes or creates a terminology issue;
- validator or CI behaviour changes;
- storyboard or production-design work begins;
- licensing or external collaboration is opened.

A risk may be marked `Accepted` only with a written rationale and named decision owner.
