# Rights and Permissions

This repository separates original project research from source texts and third-party materials.

## Do not commit by default

- full translations still under copyright;
- scans or PDFs without clear redistribution permission;
- photographs, maps, artwork or articles copied from third parties;
- proprietary production references.

Store lawfully held research copies under `private_sources/`, which is ignored by Git.

## Permitted repository content

- original research notes;
- short, properly attributed quotations within applicable legal limits;
- bibliographic metadata;
- public-domain source text where status is documented;
- original adaptation decisions, screenplay drafts and production documents.

Every source record should include rights status as one of:

- `public_domain`
- `open_licence`
- `permission_obtained`
- `citation_only`
- `status_unknown`

Unknown status means the material must not be redistributed.
